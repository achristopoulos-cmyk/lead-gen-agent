Lead Capture Popup Demo

This page demonstrates the popup form with smart triggers.

Popup Triggers

The popup will appear based on these best-practice triggers:

Exit Intent

Shows when mouse moves toward closing the tab (desktop only)

Time on Page

Shows after 30 seconds of reading

Scroll Depth

Shows after scrolling 50% of the page

Smart Cookies

Won't show again for 7 days after dismissal

How to Use

Add this single line to any webpage, just before the closing </body> tag:

<script src="https://lead-gen-agent-2qmw.onrender.com/static/popup-form.js"></script>

That's it! The popup will automatically appear based on the triggers above.

Test the Triggers

Try these actions to see the popup:

  • Wait 30 seconds - Popup will appear automatically
  • Scroll down - Popup appears at 50% scroll depth
  • Move mouse to top - Popup appears on exit intent
